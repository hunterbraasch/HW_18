WHO

We are

We help kids cope with the challenges of a parent’s deployment.

WHY

We do it

Most people focus on the deployed and forget about the kids who are left to deal with the stress.

WHAT

We do

We provide kits designed to address challenges and provide comfort at each phase of deployment.
